About schoolEat

This is a non-government institution that cooks and supply food to nearby schools in Kirinyaga Region.
We partner with Kirinyaga University to bring this service to the community.
Soon we shall be launching Community Feed Day to reach those struggling to obtain daily food and eventually establish a children's home facility. Your support will be appreciated.
